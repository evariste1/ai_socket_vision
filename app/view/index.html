BIRD EXPERT (Free Service)
 
 
BIRD EXPERT- An artificial Intelligent model that detects Bird's type based on visual observation.
 
This machine was trained on 18,000 birds.
It can recognise 150 types of birds with an accuracy > 95%.
 
Developer: Evariste G. Kana - kana@ukzn.ac.za 
 
 
Simply upload photo of  unknown bird type, and the BIRD EXPERT will help you!
